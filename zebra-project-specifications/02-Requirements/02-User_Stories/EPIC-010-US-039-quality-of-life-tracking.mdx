## Story Details
### Business Value
This story enables patients and caregivers to track, analyze, and understand quality of life metrics over time. By providing comprehensive QoL monitoring tools, it helps identify lifestyle impacts, measure treatment effectiveness, and support better health outcomes while empowering patients to take control of their overall wellbeing.

### User Story
As a patient managing a complex health condition,
I want to track and analyze various aspects of my quality of life with customizable metrics and insights,
So that I can better understand how my condition affects my daily life, make informed decisions about treatments, and communicate effectively with my healthcare team.

## Acceptance Criteria
### Scenario 1: QoL Metric Configuration
**Given** I want to track my quality of life
**When** I set up my tracking preferences
**Then** I should be able to:
- Choose from standard QoL metrics
- Create custom metrics
- Set measurement frequencies
- Configure reminders
- Define target goals
- Add personal notes
- Import existing data
- Set privacy preferences

### Scenario 2: Daily Life Impact Tracking
**Given** I am monitoring my condition's impact
**When** I record daily activities and experiences
**Then** I should be able to track:
- Physical comfort levels
- Energy and fatigue
- Sleep quality
- Emotional wellbeing
- Social interactions
- Activity limitations
- Dietary impacts
- Environmental factors

### Scenario 3: Treatment Impact Analysis
**Given** I am evaluating treatment effectiveness
**When** I review my QoL data
**Then** I should see:
- Before/after comparisons
- Trend analysis
- Treatment correlations
- Lifestyle impacts
- Success metrics
- Challenge areas
- Improvement suggestions
- Supporting research

### Scenario 4: Healthcare Team Sharing
**Given** I want to share QoL insights
**When** I prepare for healthcare visits
**Then** I should be able to:
- Generate QoL reports
- Highlight key trends
- Share specific metrics
- Control access periods
- Add context notes
- Request feedback
- Track discussions
- Update sharing preferences

### Scenario 5: Mobile and Offline Access
**Given** I need to track QoL anywhere
**When** I use the mobile interface
**Then** I should have:
- Quick entry options
- Voice input support
- Photo documentation
- Offline recording
- Data synchronization
- Emergency access
- Privacy controls
- Backup options
