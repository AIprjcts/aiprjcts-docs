## Story Details
### Business Value
This story enables the delivery of personalized, actionable health insights based on individual health data, patterns, and goals. By providing contextual recommendations and lifestyle correlations, it helps users make informed decisions about their health management.

### User Story
As a user tracking my health journey,
I want to receive personalized insights and recommendations based on my health data and patterns,
So that I can make better decisions about my health and lifestyle choices.

## Acceptance Criteria
### Scenario 1: Personalized Recommendations
**Given** I have active health tracking data
**When** I request health insights
**Then** I should receive:
- Custom recommendations based on my health profile
- Lifestyle suggestions aligned with my goals
- Actionable steps for improvement
- Progress tracking towards goals
- Alternative options when available
- Evidence-based explanations
- Difficulty levels for suggestions
- Expected outcome predictions

### Scenario 2: Lifestyle Correlation Analysis
**Given** I have multiple data sources
**When** the system analyzes my health patterns
**Then** it should:
- Identify correlations between activities and health outcomes
- Show impact of lifestyle choices
- Highlight positive and negative patterns
- Suggest beneficial behavior changes
- Provide scientific context
- Enable "what-if" scenarios
- Track intervention effectiveness
- Allow user feedback on correlations

### Scenario 3: Goal-Based Insights
**Given** I have set health goals
**When** I review my progress
**Then** the system should:
- Show progress towards goals
- Adjust recommendations based on progress
- Identify obstacles to success
- Suggest goal modifications if needed
- Celebrate achievements
- Provide motivation during setbacks
- Enable goal sharing with care team
- Generate progress reports
