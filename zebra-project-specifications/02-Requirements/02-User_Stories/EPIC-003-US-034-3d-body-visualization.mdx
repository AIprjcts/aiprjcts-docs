## Story Details
### Business Value
This story enables patients with complex conditions to better understand and communicate their health status through interactive 3D visualization. By providing intuitive visual representation of conditions and their relationships, it improves health literacy, patient-provider communication, and treatment understanding.

### User Story
As a patient with complex medical conditions affecting multiple body systems,
I want an interactive 3D visualization of my health conditions and their relationships,
So that I can better understand my health status, communicate with healthcare providers, and track changes over time.

## Acceptance Criteria
### Scenario 1: Interactive 3D Model
**Given** I am viewing my health information
**When** I access the 3D visualization
**Then** I should be able to:
- Rotate and zoom smoothly
- Pan across the model
- Toggle body systems
- Focus on regions
- View cross-sections
- Access detailed info
- Save custom views
- Share visualizations

### Scenario 2: Condition Mapping
**Given** I have multiple health conditions
**When** I view the 3D model
**Then** the system should:
- Highlight affected areas
- Show relationships
- Indicate severity
- Display progression
- Link to details
- Support multiple conditions
- Track changes
- Enable annotations

### Scenario 3: Mobile Experience
**Given** I am using a mobile device
**When** I interact with the 3D model
**Then** I should experience:
- Smooth performance
- Touch optimization
- Responsive layout
- Offline capability
- Battery efficiency
- Data optimization
- Quick loading
- Gesture support
