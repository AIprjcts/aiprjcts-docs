## Epic Overview
### Business Context
Patients and caregivers need easy access to reliable medical information to understand their health conditions better. This epic enables:
- Scientific literature search and access (PubMed, etc.)
- Personal medical library management
- AI-powered health knowledge assistant
- Universal medical term understanding
- Third-party medical knowledge integration

### Description
This epic implements a comprehensive medical knowledge system that combines:
1. Scientific literature access and management
2. Personal medical library organization
3. AI-powered health knowledge assistance
4. System-wide medical term understanding
5. Integration with trusted third-party sources
