## Story Details
### Business Value
Provide users with an intelligent AI assistant that can explain medical concepts, answer health-related questions, and help users understand complex medical information in a conversational and accessible way.

### User Story
As a patient or caregiver,
I want to interact with an AI health assistant that can explain medical concepts and answer my health-related questions,
So that I can better understand complex medical information and make informed decisions about my health.

## Acceptance Criteria
### Scenario 1: General Health Queries
**Given** I have a health-related question
**When** I interact with the AI assistant
**Then** I should receive:
- Clear, accurate answers
- Referenced information sources
- Related topic suggestions
- Visual aids when applicable
- Option to ask follow-up questions
- Simplified explanations
- Links to detailed resources
- Save conversation option

### Scenario 2: Medical Concept Explanation
**Given** I need to understand a medical concept
**When** I ask the AI assistant for an explanation
**Then** I should get:
- Layered explanations (basic to detailed)
- Visual representations
- Real-world examples
- Related concepts
- Common misconceptions
- Expert-verified information
- Further reading suggestions
- Interactive learning elements

### Scenario 3: Personalized Learning
**Given** I use the AI assistant regularly
**When** I engage in conversations
**Then** the system should:
- Remember my context
- Adapt to my knowledge level
- Reference past conversations
- Track my interests
- Suggest relevant topics
- Maintain conversation history
- Enable topic bookmarking
- Support multi-session learning
