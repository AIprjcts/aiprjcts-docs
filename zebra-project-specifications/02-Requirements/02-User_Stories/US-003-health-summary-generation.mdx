## Story Details
As a patient with a rare disease,
I want to generate comprehensive summaries of my health information,
So that I can easily share relevant information with new healthcare providers or during emergencies.

## Business Value
- Improves communication with healthcare providers
- Reduces time spent explaining medical history
- Ensures critical information is readily available
- Facilitates emergency care

## Acceptance Criteria
1. User can select information to include in summary
2. System generates summaries in multiple formats (PDF, digital)
3. Summaries include key health metrics and history
4. User can customize summary templates
5. Generated summaries are clearly formatted
6. Critical information is highlighted
7. Summaries can be easily shared
8. Version history is maintained
