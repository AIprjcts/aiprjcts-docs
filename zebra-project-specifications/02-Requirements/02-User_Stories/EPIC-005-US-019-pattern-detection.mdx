## Story Details
### Business Value
This story empowers patients and caregivers with comprehensive AI-powered health analytics, combining pattern detection, predictive insights, and research integration. By providing advanced analysis capabilities with patient control over privacy and sharing, it enables better understanding of health trends, supports proactive care through early intervention, and facilitates evidence-based decision-making while maintaining patient autonomy. The system combines temporal progression tracking with deep pattern analysis and predictive modeling to optimize patient outcomes through personalized insights and collaborative care.

### User Story
As a patient or caregiver managing complex health conditions,
I want comprehensive control over AI-powered health analysis, including pattern detection, predictions, and research insights,
So that I can better understand my health journey, make informed decisions, and selectively collaborate with my healthcare team while maintaining privacy control.
