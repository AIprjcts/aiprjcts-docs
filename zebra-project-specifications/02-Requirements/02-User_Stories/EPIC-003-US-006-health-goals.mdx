## Story Details
### Business Value
This story enables patients to set, track, and visualize their health goals through interactive dashboards and progress tracking tools. This leads to better engagement in their health journey, informed decision-making, and improved health outcomes through visual feedback and milestone tracking.

### User Story
As a patient managing my health journey,
I want to set, track, and visualize progress towards my health goals,
So that I can monitor improvements and make informed decisions about my care.

## Acceptance Criteria
### Scenario 1: Goal Visualization
**Given** I am tracking my health goals
**When** I view my dashboard
**Then** the system should provide:
- Interactive goal progress dashboards
- Visual milestone tracking
- Progress trend charts
- Achievement indicators
- Comparative analysis views

### Scenario 2: Timeline Management
**Given** I am monitoring my progress
**When** I access the timeline view
**Then** the system should display:
- Goal timeline visualization
- Progress history views
- Milestone markers
- Event correlation display
- Time-based filtering

### Scenario 3: Data Analysis
**Given** I am analyzing my health data
**When** I review my metrics
**Then** the system should show:
- Health metrics charts
- Progress indicators
- Status summaries
- Target vs. actual comparisons
- Trend analysis graphs
