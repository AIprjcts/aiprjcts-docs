## Story Details
As a patient with a complex condition,
I want the system to identify patterns and correlations in my health data,
So that I can better understand my condition triggers and progression.

## Business Value
- Enables early detection
- Improves understanding
- Supports prevention
- Facilitates treatment optimization

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Detect patterns in:
   - Symptoms
   - Triggers
   - Treatment responses
   - Vital signs
2. Correlation analysis between:
   - Medications and symptoms
   - Activities and health status
   - Environmental factors
3. Pattern visualization
4. Confidence levels for findings
5. Healthcare provider validation
6. Pattern tracking over time
7. Custom pattern monitoring
8. Alert system for new patterns

## Implementation Details
### Technical Requirements
