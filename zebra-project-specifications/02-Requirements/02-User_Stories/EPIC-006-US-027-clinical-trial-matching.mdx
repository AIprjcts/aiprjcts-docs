## Story Details
### Business Value
This story enables patients and healthcare providers to efficiently search for and view external clinical trial data through a web browser, facilitating informed decision-making and increasing awareness of available research opportunities.

### User Story
As a patient or healthcare provider,
I want to search for and view external clinical trial data in a web browser,
So that I can easily find relevant trials and understand their details to make informed decisions.

## Acceptance Criteria
### Scenario 1: Search Clinical Trials
**Given** I am looking for clinical trials
**When** I use the search feature
**Then** I should be able to:
- Enter search criteria (e.g., condition, location)
- Filter results by relevance
- Sort results by date or location

### Scenario 2: View Trial Details
**Given** I have found a trial of interest
**When** I select a trial
**Then** I should see:
- Study overview
- Eligibility criteria
- Location details
- Contact information
