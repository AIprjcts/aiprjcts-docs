## Story Details
### Business Value
This feature provides patients and caregivers with simplified decision support by offering personalized health advice, treatment options, and lifestyle recommendations based on their health data. It empowers users to make informed decisions about their health management.

### User Story
As a patient or caregiver,
I want a decision support system that provides personalized health advice and options,
So that I can make informed decisions about health management and care strategies.

## Acceptance Criteria
### Scenario 1: Personalized Health Advice
**Given** I have health data available
**When** I request health advice
**Then** the system should:
- Provide personalized health tips
- Suggest lifestyle changes
- Offer dietary recommendations
- Highlight potential health risks
- Suggest questions for healthcare providers
- Provide context for advice
- Allow feedback on advice relevance
- Enable saving and sharing of advice

### Scenario 2: Treatment Options Overview
**Given** I am considering treatment options
**When** I explore available treatments
**Then** the system should:
- List common treatment options
- Explain benefits and risks
- Provide success stories
- Suggest questions to ask providers
- Highlight alternative therapies
- Offer cost considerations
- Enable comparison of options
- Allow user feedback on options

### Scenario 3: Lifestyle and Wellness Recommendations
**Given** I want to improve my health
**When** I seek wellness recommendations
**Then** the system should:
- Suggest exercise routines
- Recommend stress management techniques
- Offer sleep improvement tips
- Provide mental health resources
- Suggest community support groups
- Highlight wellness apps and tools
- Enable goal setting and tracking
- Allow sharing of wellness plans
