## Epic Overview
### Business Context
Healthcare needs are global, and users require access to health information in their preferred language and cultural context. Key aspects include:
- Multi-language support for global accessibility
- Cultural adaptation of health information
- Region-specific healthcare standards
- Local medical terminology
- Regulatory compliance across regions

### Features
- Multi-language interface
- Cultural adaptation system
- Regional healthcare standards
- Local medical terminology
- Time zone management
- Unit conversion (metrics)
- Regional formatting
- Language-specific content
- Cultural health practices
- Local regulatory compliance
