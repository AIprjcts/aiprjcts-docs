## Story Details
### Business Value
Enable instant understanding of medical terminology throughout the application by providing context-aware explanations and definitions, similar to a "Grammarly for medical terms", improving health literacy and user comprehension.

### User Story
As a user of the health platform,
I want instant access to medical term explanations anywhere in the application,
So that I can understand medical terminology without losing context or interrupting my workflow.

## Acceptance Criteria
### Scenario 1: Term Detection and Highlighting
**Given** I am viewing any content in the application
**When** medical terms are present
**Then** the system should:
- Automatically detect medical terms
- Subtly highlight identified terms
- Indicate terms have explanations available
- Allow enabling/disabling highlighting
- Remember my preferences
- Adjust to my knowledge level
- Learn from my interaction history

### Scenario 2: Term Explanation Access
**Given** I encounter a highlighted medical term
**When** I hover over or click the term
**Then** I should see:
- Brief definition in a tooltip
- Option for more detailed explanation
- Visual aids when available
- Pronunciation guide
- Usage examples
- Related terms
- Source references
- Save option
