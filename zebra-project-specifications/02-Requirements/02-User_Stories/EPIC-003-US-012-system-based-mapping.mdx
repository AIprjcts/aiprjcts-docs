## Story Details
### Business Value
This story enables healthcare providers to better understand and treat complex multi-system conditions through AI-powered visualization and analysis. By organizing health data by body systems and providing intelligent insights, it improves clinical decision-making, treatment planning, and patient outcomes while supporting evidence-based practice.

### User Story
As a healthcare provider treating patients with complex multi-system conditions,
I want to visualize and analyze patient health data organized by body systems with AI-powered insights,
So that I can better understand system interactions, identify patterns, and make informed treatment decisions.

## Acceptance Criteria
### Scenario 1: System-Based Data Organization
**Given** I am analyzing a patient's health data
**When** I select a body system
**Then** I should see:
- Active conditions
- Related symptoms
- Treatment history
- System trends
- Cross-relationships
- AI patterns
- Key measurements
- Clinical notes

### Scenario 2: Multi-System Analysis
**Given** I am investigating complex conditions
**When** I analyze multiple systems
**Then** I should receive:
- System relationships
- Interaction patterns
- Causal links
- Treatment impacts
- Research insights
- Dependencies
- Risk factors
- Clinical guidance

### Scenario 3: Temporal Analysis
**Given** I need to understand progression
**When** I view data over time
**Then** I should be able to:
- Track changes
- See patterns
- Compare systems
- Analyze impacts
- View trends
- Generate reports
- Set alerts
- Share insights

### Scenario 4: Clinical Decision Support
**Given** I am making treatment decisions
**When** I review system data
**Then** I should receive:
- Evidence-based advice
- Similar cases
- Outcome predictions
- Risk assessments
- Research links
- Expert insights
- Treatment options
- Safety alerts
