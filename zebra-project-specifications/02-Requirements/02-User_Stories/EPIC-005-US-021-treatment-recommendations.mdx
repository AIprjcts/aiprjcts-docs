## Story Details
### Business Value
This story empowers patients with AI-powered insights about treatment options while maintaining control over their healthcare decisions. By providing research-based evidence and facilitating meaningful provider discussions, it enables informed decision-making and improved patient-provider collaboration while ensuring patient privacy and autonomy.

### User Story
As a patient or caregiver managing complex health conditions,
I want AI-powered insights about treatment options with research-based evidence,
So that I can make informed decisions about my care and have meaningful discussions with my healthcare team.

## Acceptance Criteria
### Scenario 1: Treatment Research
**Given** I am researching treatments
**When** I request insights
**Then** I should receive:
- Option details
- Success rates
- Risk levels
- Cost data
- Side effects
- Alternatives
- Life impacts
- Time frames

### Scenario 2: Research Integration
**Given** I want evidence support
**When** I explore options
**Then** I should access:
- New research
- Trial data
- Patient stories
- Guidelines
- Case studies
- Success data
- Quality info
- Evidence ranks

### Scenario 3: Provider Sharing
**Given** I want provider input
**When** I share insights
**Then** I should control:
- Provider access
- Info filters
- Discussion notes
- Questions
- Decisions
- Progress
- Outcomes
- Experiences

### Scenario 4: Privacy Management
**Given** I want data privacy
**When** I manage insights
**Then** I should be able to:
- Set permissions
- Control access
- Track sharing
- Revoke rights
- Monitor use
- Get alerts
- Ensure privacy
- Maintain control
