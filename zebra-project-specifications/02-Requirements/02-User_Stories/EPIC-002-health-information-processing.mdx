## Epic Overview
### Business Context
Patients and caregivers often struggle to understand complex medical terminology and health information, leading to confusion and potential misunderstandings about their health conditions. This challenge affects their ability to make informed decisions and effectively communicate with healthcare providers. The Zebra platform needs to:
- Translate complex medical terminology into patient-friendly language
- Generate meaningful health summaries for different purposes
- Support multiple languages for diverse user populations
- Enable clear communication with healthcare providers
- Provide educational content to improve health literacy

### Features
- Medical terminology translation
- Automated health summaries
- Multilingual support
- Context-aware explanations
- Interactive health education
- AI-powered health insights
- Appointment preparation and management
- Visit summary generation
