## Epic Overview
Enable users to understand and process complex medical information through automated summaries, translations, and provider updates.

## Business Context
Patients and caregivers need help understanding complex medical terminology and generating meaningful summaries of their health information for various purposes.

## Strategic Alignment
- Supports core vision of making health information accessible
- Enables better patient understanding
- Facilitates communication with healthcare providers
- Supports multilingual capabilities

## Success Metrics
- User comprehension rates
- Summary generation usage
- Translation accuracy
- Provider communication efficiency
- User satisfaction with information clarity

## Value Streams
1. Medical Information Translation
2. Health Summary Generation
3. Provider Communication
4. Knowledge Management

## User Activities
1. Health Summary Creation
   - Description: Generate comprehensive health summaries for different purposes
   - Related Personas: PER-001, PER-002, PER-003
   - Priority: High

2. Medical Translation
   - Description: Translate medical terms into patient-friendly language
   - Related Personas: All patient and caregiver personas
   - Priority: High

3. Provider Updates
   - Description: Generate and share updates with healthcare providers
   - Related Personas: PER-001, PER-002, PER-006, PER-007
   - Priority: Medium

4. Automated Data Analysis
   - Description: Implement AI-driven analysis to identify key health trends and insights from patient data.
   - Related Personas: PER-003, PER-004
   - Priority: High

5. Interactive Health Education
   - Description: Provide interactive educational content to help users understand complex medical information.
   - Related Personas: PER-001, PER-005
   - Priority: Medium
