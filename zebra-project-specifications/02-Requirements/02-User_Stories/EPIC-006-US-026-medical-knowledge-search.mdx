## Story Details
### Business Value
This story empowers patients with rare diseases and complex conditions to better understand their health through AI-powered medical knowledge search. By providing natural language understanding and personalized explanations, it enables informed decision-making and improved health literacy, ultimately leading to better health outcomes.

### User Story
As a patient with a rare disease or complex condition,
I want to search and understand medical information using natural language and receive AI-powered explanations,
So that I can better comprehend my health conditions, treatments, and make informed decisions about my care.

## Acceptance Criteria
### Scenario 1: Natural Language Search
**Given** I am a patient seeking medical information
**When** I enter a natural language query
**Then** the system should:
- Process natural language
- Understand medical terms
- Consider context
- Match semantically
- Support multiple languages
- Enable voice search
- Suggest corrections
- Show related terms

### Scenario 2: Intelligent Results
**Given** the system has processed my search
**When** results are displayed
**Then** I should see:
- Ranked results
- Filtered content
- Personalized suggestions
- Related concepts
- Visual content
- Credibility scores
- Source information
- Quick summaries

### Scenario 3: Knowledge Presentation
**Given** I am viewing search results
**When** I explore the information
**Then** I should access:
- Simple explanations
- Technical details
- Visual diagrams
- Research links
- Treatment info
- Trial data
- Expert insights
- Related resources

### Scenario 4: Interactive Features
**Given** I want to explore medical concepts
**When** I use interactive features
**Then** I should be able to:
- Explore terms
- View relationships
- See knowledge graphs
- Interact with 3D models
- View timelines
- Map symptoms
- Save information
- Share findings
