## Story Details
As a patient with a rare disease,
I want to visualize my health conditions on a 3D body model,
So that I can better understand and communicate about my health issues with healthcare providers.

## Business Value
- Improves understanding of health conditions
- Facilitates communication with providers
- Enhances patient education
- Supports better health tracking

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Interactive 3D human body model is available
2. User can rotate and zoom the model
3. Body systems can be toggled on/off
4. Conditions are accurately mapped to body locations
5. Medical terms have patient-friendly explanations
6. Model supports highlighting specific areas
7. Information can be filtered by body region
8. Performance is smooth on standard devices
