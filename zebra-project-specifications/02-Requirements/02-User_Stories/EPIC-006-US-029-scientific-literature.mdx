## Story Details
### Business Value
Enable patients and caregivers to search, access, and manage scientific medical literature, creating a personal medical library for better understanding of their health conditions through evidence-based research.

### User Story
As a patient or caregiver,
I want to search and save relevant medical research papers and documents,
So that I can build a personal medical library and better understand my health conditions through scientific evidence.

## Acceptance Criteria
### Scenario 1: Scientific Literature Search
**Given** I need to find medical research papers
**When** I use the search functionality
**Then** I should be able to:
- Search PubMed and other databases
- Filter by relevance and date
- See paper abstracts
- Access full texts when available
- View citation information
- See related papers
- Save interesting papers
- Share papers with others

### Scenario 2: Personal Library Management
**Given** I have saved research papers
**When** I access my medical library
**Then** I should be able to:
- Organize papers in collections
- Add personal notes
- Tag documents
- Create reading lists
- Track read/unread status
- Export citations
- Share collections
- Sync across devices
