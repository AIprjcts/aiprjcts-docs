## Story Details
As a healthcare provider,
I want to receive AI-powered predictions about potential health issues,
So that I can take proactive measures in patient care.

## Business Value
- Enables preventive care
- Reduces complications
- Improves outcomes
- Supports early intervention

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Generate predictions for:
   - Condition progression
   - Potential complications
   - Treatment responses
   - Health risks
2. Provide prediction confidence levels
3. Show supporting evidence
4. Include relevant research
5. Suggest preventive measures
6. Track prediction accuracy
7. Allow provider feedback
8. Integration with care plans

## Implementation Details
### Technical Requirements

- Research integration
