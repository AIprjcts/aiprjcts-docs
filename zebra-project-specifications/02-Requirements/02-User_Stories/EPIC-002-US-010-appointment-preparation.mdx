## Story Details
### Business Value
This story enables patients to better prepare for medical appointments by organizing relevant health information, preparing questions, and gathering necessary documents. This leads to more productive healthcare visits, better informed discussions, and improved health outcomes through structured preparation.

### User Story
As a patient preparing for a medical appointment,
I want the system to help me prepare relevant health information and questions,
So that I can have more productive and informed discussions with my healthcare providers.

## Acceptance Criteria
### Scenario 1: Preparation Setup
**Given** I have an upcoming medical appointment
**When** I start preparation
**Then** the system should:
- Generate a preparation checklist
- Present relevant health history
- Show recent health changes
- List current medications
- Display recent test results
- Suggest relevant questions
- Enable document uploads
- Create a visit timeline

### Scenario 2: Information Organization
**Given** I am gathering health information
**When** I review my health data
**Then** the system should:
- Show organized health history
- Display vital trends
- Present symptom logs
- List treatment plans
- Show provider notes
- Display care goals
- Enable information filtering
- Support custom organization

### Scenario 3: Visit Planning
**Given** I am finalizing appointment preparation
**When** I create my visit plan
**Then** the system should:
- Enable question customization
- Support note-taking
- Allow priority setting
- Provide time management tools
- Enable sharing options
- Support export functions
- Set up reminders
- Track follow-up items
