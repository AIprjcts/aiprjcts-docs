## Epic Overview
### Business Context
Users need access to reliable health information and educational resources to:
- Better understand their health conditions
- Make informed healthcare decisions
- Learn about treatments and medications
- Access reliable medical information
- Stay updated on health best practices

### Features
- Comprehensive medical knowledge base
- Interactive health education modules
- Personalized learning paths
- Medical terminology dictionary
- Treatment information database
- Drug interaction checker
- Health condition guides
- Latest medical research summaries
- Patient education system
- Learning progress tracking
- Educational content management
- Multimedia learning resources
