## Epic Overview
Provide a comprehensive, up-to-date medical knowledge base that helps users understand conditions, treatments, and medical terminology, with special focus on rare diseases.

## Business Context
Users need reliable, understandable medical information to make informed decisions about their health care, particularly for rare and complex conditions.

## Strategic Alignment
- Supports patient education
- Enhances health literacy
- Facilitates informed decisions
- Supports research integration

## Success Metrics
