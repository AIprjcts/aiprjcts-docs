## Story Details
### Business Value
Enable users to engage with the platform community, share experiences, and provide peer support while building a knowledge base of user-generated content.

### User Story
As a platform user,
I want to participate in the community support system,
So that I can share experiences, ask questions, and help other users while building collective knowledge.

## Acceptance Criteria
### Scenario 1: Community Interaction
**Given** I am a platform user
**When** I access the community section
**Then** I should be able to:
- Create discussion topics
- Reply to existing threads
- Share experiences
- Vote on helpful content
- Follow interesting topics

### Scenario 2: Knowledge Contribution
**Given** I have valuable information to share
**When** I contribute to the community
**Then** I should be able to:
- Create knowledge articles
- Add best practices
- Share tips and tricks
- Tag content appropriately
- Receive feedback
