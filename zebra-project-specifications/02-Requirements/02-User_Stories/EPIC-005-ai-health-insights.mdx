## Epic Overview
### Business Context
Healthcare data is complex and often difficult to interpret. Patients and caregivers need intelligent tools to:
- Understand health trends and patterns
- Receive personalized health recommendations
- Identify potential health risks early
- Track progress towards health goals
- Make informed healthcare decisions

### Features
- AI-powered health trend analysis
- Personalized health insights
- Early warning system for health risks
- Progress tracking and visualization
- Natural language health summaries
- Predictive health analytics
- Integration with medical knowledge bases
- Customizable health dashboards
- AI health assistant
- Contextual help and guidance
