## Epic Overview
Leverage artificial intelligence to provide meaningful insights, detect patterns, and generate recommendations based on patient health data.

## Business Context
Users need intelligent analysis of their health data to identify patterns, predict potential issues, and receive personalized recommendations for better health management.

## Strategic Alignment
- Supports proactive health management
- Enables early detection of issues
- Provides personalized insights
- Facilitates data-driven decisions

## Success Metrics
- Pattern detection accuracy
- Prediction reliability
- User engagement with insights
- Healthcare provider adoption
- Impact on health outcomes

## Value Streams

2. Predictive Analytics
3. Treatment Recommendations
4. Research Support
