## Epic Overview
### Business Context
Healthcare systems must maintain strict compliance with various regulations and maintain comprehensive audit trails. Key aspects include:
- HIPAA, GDPR, and other healthcare regulations compliance
- Comprehensive audit trail maintenance
- Privacy and consent management
- Compliance monitoring and reporting
- Documentation and policy management
- Regular compliance audits and reviews

### Features
1. Regulatory Compliance
   - HIPAA compliance framework
   - GDPR compliance tools
   - Regional healthcare standards
   - Policy management system
   - Compliance documentation
   - Regulatory updates tracking

2. Audit System
   - Comprehensive audit logging
   - Activity tracking
   - Access monitoring
   - Change management
   - Audit report generation
   - Historical data retention

3. Privacy Controls
   - Consent management
   - Privacy settings
   - Data handling policies
   - Rights management
   - Privacy documentation
   - Compliance verification
